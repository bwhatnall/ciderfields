    Our Philosophy

    We are a small, fully registered professional breeder. Our pups are healthy, confident pups, with a loving, people focussed temperament. Their parents are comprehensively genetically health tested, with the Orivet Full Breed Profile, to help ensure your pup remains healthy and clear of genetic diseases. Our dogs are much loved and well cared for each step of the way. Pups are firstly born and raised in our home, then safely explore the natural environment, with play, daily human love and attention as they get older. We feed a supplemented raw diet for an optimal start to life.
